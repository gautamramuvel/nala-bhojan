About Us

Developed specifically for our corporate clients, we offer range of catering services for the corporate sector, which focuses on the needs of busy professionals. We realize that businesses are looking for a caterer that offers a high quality product, timely service , and a variety of menu options.
Safety & Hygiene, Delivery Excellence , Customer Delight and Integrity form our foundation pillars and it is with these core values we have created a strong partnership with industries and corporate that demand world class services at a nominal cost.
Our Corporate menu features an assortment of creative items ranging from comforting Idlies, crunchy Dosas and mouth-watering South Indian, North Indian Meals, scrumptious Chats & Sandwiches to hearty, delicious appetizers and sinfully amazing desserts.

Compliance
