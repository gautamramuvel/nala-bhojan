Our Special Dishes

Iyer & Iyengar Meals


Nala’s orthodox Brahmin meals with great attention to cleanliness, hygiene and the right mix of flavour, nutrition, texture and variety is the most popular of our Menu. Be it Vadakalai or ThenkalaiIyengar from any part of South and Iyers from Thirunelveli, North Arcot or even Palagat… Nala is an expert in all of the cooking styles! Trust your Weddings & Celebrations at home with Nala and taste happiness!

South Indian Veg Specialities


Nala’s specializes in South Indian delicacies ranging from Tamilnadu, Andhra, Telangana, Kerala and Karnataka. Sambhar, Rasam, Kootu, Puliyotharai, Paruppuada, Vathalkozhambu, Ennaikathirikai Kozhambu Of Tam, Bisibele Bath, Vegetable Sagu, Allugedda, Mango Chutney of Kannadika, Malabaravial, Molagootal, Erissery, Olan of Mallu, Gonkura, Guttivangayakoora, Pappulu Podi of Telugu… Nala masters all. So next time you have a requirement order from ‘Nala’ and taste happiness!

North Indian Veg Delicacies


Nala’s Founder & Chief Chef Dr Sivakumar’s proficiency is not just in blending the southern spice but also the flavors of North. From simple comfort food like Dal Tadka and RajmaChawal to butter & cream rich Paneer Butter Masala, MalaiKofta, dal makhani, pulav varieties, briyani varieties, tantoori cuisines, we make it all. Come choose your favourite North Indian dish for your next order, take a bite and you know you are in for a gastronomic joyride that will blow you away!

Dosas


An indispensible South Indian classic, dosa is universal in India. Just a bite of this thin wafer like crepes with Sambhar or chutney or any masala transports you to heaven. Do you know that Dosa is listed in world’s one of the 50 most delicious food and Top 10 tasty foods of the world? Nala’sDosa creations include over 300 varieties of this versatile dish – Pizza Dosa, Burger Dosa, Mexican Dosa or Chinese Dosa... Plan for a variety dosa counter for your event and amaze your guests with scrumptiousness.

Sweets & Desserts


It is not without a reason when people across the globe say that we Indian take our food seriously! Every meal is a celebration and has to be ended with a dessert. From mouth melting Mysore Pak to ghee laddu, sweet pongal, jangiri and payasams of South to hot malpua with decadent rabdi, melt-in-the-mouth makhanmalai, kesar flavoured phirni, rasamalai, rasakulla, gulabjamuns, halwas, burfis and more… the wide range of irresistible treats are sure to bowl you over. Taste them at Nala and taste happiness!

International Flavors


When our motto is to delight our customers with delicious food then why restrict to just Indian cuisines. Chinese, Italian, Mexican, Mangolian or Thai..pizza, pasta, sandwiches, fried rice and curry varieties from Asia… Nala does it for you! Try any of these for your event and taste happiness!
