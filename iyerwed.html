Wedding

Weddings

The traditional Indian wedding is about two families being brought together socially, with as much emphasis placed on the families coming closer as the married couple. The wedding rituals and food play a very vital. At Nala we understand the efforts and emotions behind a wedding and help you organize the event to perfection. Decide the wedding date & venue and call Nala Catering. They do it all for you!

Iyer & Iyangar Wedding

Among the most ritualistic of Indian weddings, the traditional Brahmin weddings stand apart. The changing times has not changed their affair that is full of camaraderie, fun and rituals. After all cut shorts, it is still a grand three day affair! Nala is a specialist in the making of South Indian Brahmin Weddings. Right from MandapaKolam (Rangoli) to KattuSadham we organize and guide you through the event. Flawless grand ceremony and tasty high quality food is Nala’s assurance.
